How to Use This Dashboard

This visualization provides a comprehensive overview of your sales deals, including competitors, customer ecosystem, deal size, opportunity size, and the decision-making unit.

Deal Relationships Tab

The relationship visualization shows how all elements of the sales opportunity are interconnected:

  • Central Node: The deal itself
  • Decision Making Unit: Stakeholders with different shapes based on role (diamond=executive, square=technical, etc.)
  • Customer Ecosystem: Consultants, system integrators, and other external entities
  • Competitors: Companies competing for the same opportunity

Node size indicates influence level, and colors indicate sentiment or threat level.

Deal Metrics Tab

The metrics visualization provides quantitative analysis:

  • Deal Value vs. Opportunity Size: Current deal compared to total potential
  • Win Probability: Likelihood of winning the deal
  • Stakeholder Sentiment: Distribution of stakeholder attitudes
  • Competitor Positioning: Relative strength of competitors

Interactive Features

You can interact with the visualization in several ways:

  • Hover over any node to see detailed information
  • Click on legend items to show/hide specific categories
  • Zoom in/out using mouse wheel or pinch gestures
  • Pan by clicking and dragging

Best Practices for Using This Dashboard

  • Update the visualization before important sales meetings
  • Use the relationship view to identify key influencers and potential champions
  • Use the metrics view to track deal progress and competitor positioning
  • Share the visualization with your sales team to align on strategy
  • Focus on converting neutral stakeholders to supporters
  • Identify and mitigate the influence of competitors on key stakeholders
  • Leverage positive relationships in the customer ecosystem
